NAO-Headtouch-test

Initial overview of the NAO robot's capabilities.


A presentation routine was developed to showcase the robot's movements within a limited range, utilizing three sensors located on its head. The associated functions, such as arm movements, positions, and LED color changes, were programmed using Choregraphe software.

Word recognition with the NAO robot.


A ‘Speech Reco’ test routine was developed to identify key words that prompt the robot to follow specific instructions. The identification of these words was handled using a ‘Switch Case’ operator to control the movements.
